About Neurovia

Neurovia is a modern smart city located to the east of Cairo, within Egypt‚Äôs New Administrative Capital. It was developed in the year 2025 as part of the country's vision to build sustainable and technologically advanced cities. Neurovia aims to provide an integrated environment that relies on the latest technologies in various sectors such as transportation, energy, digital services, education, and healthcare. Key features of the city include the use of clean energy sources, a smart transportation system that reduces congestion and pollution, a high-speed internet network that covers the entire city, and educational and healthcare centers powered by artificial intelligence. Neurovia seeks to be a leading model for the future of urban development in the Middle East and North Africa.
